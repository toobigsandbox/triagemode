The Presentation Script

STEP 01

The Script (Voice)

"Look at this rectangle. We call this side the 'Base' and this side the 'Height'."

The Action (Hands)

Place the yellow rectangle on the table and trace the perimeter with your finger.

Going Deeper?

Developmental Link

Why this is for the "Second Plane" child: They love big work and big numbers.

The Math Connection

How this leads to A = ½(b×h) without needing a calculator.

Watch Out!

Don't call the slanted side of a parallelogram the 'height'. That's a trap!
