Guide's Field Notes

Long Division with Stamps

Quotative ("Grouping") Division

The Setup

  • Stamp Game: Full set.
  • The Stone: A small, non-hierarchical object (pebble, button) to track place value. Crucial for focus!
  • Paper: Grid paper to write the problem before starting.

Watch Out!

Zeroes: The skittles are place holders. If you have a zero in the divisor, use a place marker (like the colored disks) to hold the spot, even if no stamps go there initially.

Pedagogy: "Calibration"

"Use 1-digit divisors first. Not to bore them, but to 'calibrate' their understanding. Can they exchange? Do they know where to write the answer? Once confirmed, move quickly to multi-digit division."

Lesson Snapshot

  • Goal: Reinforce regrouping/exchange in division.
  • Key Tool: The Stone Marker (tracks the active category).
  • Process: Share Thousands -> Exchange remainder -> Share Hundreds -> etc.
  • Answer: The quantity under ONE skittle (Unit).
